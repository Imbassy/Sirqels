Frequently
asked questions

Projects

How long does a typical web design project take at Imbassy?

Our projects vary in duration based on complexity and client requirements. Typically, a standard website can take anywhere from 4 to 8 weeks, while more intricate sites may take longer. We'll provide a more specific timeline once we understand your needs.

Can Imbassy handle e-commerce and other specialized website functionalities?

Absolutely! We have experience in designing a variety of websites, including e-commerce platforms, portfolios, blogs, and more. Whatever your requirement, we're here to help.

Budget

Do you have package rates or is each project quoted individually?

Each project is unique, so while we do have standard packages, we often provide tailored quotations based on your specific requirements and goals.

Is there a maintenance cost associated with the websites you design?

Maintenance costs vary depending on the level of ongoing support you need. We offer several maintenance and support packages, and we can discuss the best fit for your site and budget.

What is the average cost of a website in 2023?

In 2023, the average cost of a website varies greatly based on its complexity, features, and design requirements. For a basic website, prices typically start at around ~$11,000, while more complex sites with custom functionalities can go beyond $100,000+. It's best to discuss specific needs for a tailored estimate.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer flexible financing options for your new website build.

Customer Support

How do I reach out if I face issues with my website post-launch?

We pride ourselves on ImbassyCare. Should you encounter any problems or require updates, our support team is just an email or Slack message away.

Do you offer training on how to manage and update the website once it's built?

Yes, we offer training sessions for clients to familiarize them with the website's backend, ensuring you can make basic updates and changes as needed.
