Why Webflow Apps?

Become the part of Webflow App Marketplace

Webflow Apps is a useful tool that can simplify the creation of websites but also opens up new possibilities for the creation of innovative apps. With your unique Webflow App, you can become part of the Webflow App Marketplace and make your company stand out from the crowd.

What is a Webflow App?

The Webflow App is software or functionality developed by a third party for use in the Webflow platform. By adding a Webflow App to your website, you bridge the gap between your website and external pages and combine them for a more tailored experience.

Give new values to your website

In designer mode, you can use Webflow Apps to add new values to your website, activate desired fields, and give them improved functionality. By using existing Webflow Apps or building new ones, multiple benefits for developers, designers, and users are guaranteed.

Frequently
asked questions

Are Webflow Apps safe to use?

Webflow platform experts are constantly working on improvements to ensure that all data is secured and protected to the maximum. This includes regular monitoring of the actions that take place on the Webflow platform, and preventing malicious acts. All Webflow Apps you find on the Webflow marketplace are approved and verified by the Webflow platform.

Can I use more than one Webflow app at the same time?

You can use as many Webflow apps as you need to enhance your website. The combination of Webflow apps allows you to create websites with more diverse features that offer multiple benefits to your users.

What is the Webflow app price?

The use of Webflow apps within the Webflow platform is free of charge and every Webflow user has access to them. However, each app has its price, which is set by the developers and can vary from app to app, depending on the app license purchased. For the price of creating your app, contact Imbassy and get your consultation to learn the entire process of creating the Webflow app.

How can I create a custom Webflow app?

If you want to create a custom Webflow app, contact us. Imbassy will guide you through the entire process of creating, developing, and reviewing your Webflow app.
