Dynamic Redesign - Migration to Modern CMS

Overview

Let’s start with a little background on Instabug, Instabug is a trailblazing software company that offers a wide range of cutting-edge solutions to empower mobile app developers to provide their users with seamless and engaging experiences. 

With Instabug's lineup of tools, developers can quickly and easily identify and address bugs, monitor app performance, track crashes, engage in in-app chats, and gather user feedback via surveys, all within the context of their app.

In today's hyper-competitive mobile app market, Instabug provides an essential advantage to developers looking to stay ahead of the curve. 

With its user-friendly interface and robust feature set, Instabug makes it easy for developers to gain deep insights into the behavior of their apps and take action to improve them.

Creative Collaboration and Agile Solutions

Instabug faced a critical challenge that threatened to hamper its growth trajectory. The issue at hand was a rigid, hard-coded website that restricted their ability to expand their business to new heights. 

This technical limitation was posing a serious hurdle in their efforts to keep pace with the growing demands of their users. Despite their best efforts, Instabug was struggling to find a solution to this challenge. 

The hard-coded website was constraining their ability to adapt and innovate, and they were in danger of losing their competitive edge. They knew they had to find a way to scale their business and meet the changing needs of their users.

In today's fast-paced digital world, having a website that is scalable and user-friendly is essential to the success of any business. 

This is why when Instabug's Chief Marketing Officer and internal marketing team reached out to Sirqels for a solution to their website scaling needs, our team was more than happy to help.

Sirqels specializes in providing businesses with customized solutions to help them achieve their online goals. 

After assessing Instabug's site issues and pain points, our team determined that the best solution would be to transition their website from WordPress to Webflow - a powerful platform that offers powerful web development and design capabilities.

But our team didn't stop there. We also migrated Instabug's blog from WP under a subdomain to Almirante, integrating it into the main site and connecting it to the main domain. 

This move not only improved the overall user experience but also allowed for SEO to be fully optimized, ensuring that Instabug's website would rank higher in search engine results.

Are you ready to take your website to new heights?
